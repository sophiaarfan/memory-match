⊹ ࣪ ˖Emoji Memory Match ࣪ ˖⊹

You will be given 5 seconds to view a set of cards, each having an emoji displayed. The cards are then flipped over so the emoji's are hidden, you have to select two cards at a time, aiming to get a matching pair based on what you remember from when the cards were face-up.

Wrong matches will flash red and add 2 seconds to your timer.

Scores are calculated taking into account how many moves it took you to get all matches, and the time you took to complete the puzzle.

Scores and personal bests are tracked separately per difficulty.

Difficulties:

Easy: 4 pairs to find | Medium: 8 pairs to find | Hard: 16 pairs to find
